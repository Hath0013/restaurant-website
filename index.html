Menu

Culinary and baking students prepare all the food, including our exclusive line of La Dolce Vita preserves and Dirty Duck Soaps. You’ll find everything from soups and salads to complete meals, breads, cakes and pastries and more.

Space

Enjoy your freshly-made meal while relaxing in our calm, modern atmosphere. Bring a laptop to study, a book to read or listen to some of your favourite music.
